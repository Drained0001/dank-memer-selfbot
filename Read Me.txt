Usage Tips:
Use on a alt
Dont spam in multiple channels (will lead to blacklist)

How To Use:
Step 1. Put your discord token in the last line of main.py
Step 2. Go to cog files and change the "daguildid" variable to the guild you will be auto farming in
step 3. Go to the cog files again and change the "channel" variable to the channel you will be auto farming in
step 4. Run main.py and use dm!help to see what to do

Install all requirements in requirements.txt
